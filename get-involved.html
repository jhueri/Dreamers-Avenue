Volunteer Opportunities

We have various volunteer opportunities that match different skills and availability. Volunteers can help with:

  • Workshop facilitation
  • Event planning and coordination
  • Content creation (guides, resources)
  • Social media management
  • Administrative support
  • Translation services

Volunteer commitments range from one-time events to ongoing weekly roles. All volunteers receive training and support from our team.

Partner With Us

We collaborate with organizations that share our commitment to educational equity and supporting undocumented students. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Corporate Sponsorships for events and programs
  • Workshop Collaborations with industry experts
  • Internship Pathways for our students
  • Awareness Campaigns to amplify our mission

Our partners benefit from increased visibility in the community, employee engagement opportunities, and the chance to make a meaningful impact on educational equity.
