Our Mission

Dreamers Avenue is dedicated to empowering undocumented high school students to pursue higher education by providing mentorship, resources, and community support throughout the college application process.


We believe that every student, regardless of immigration status, deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to achieve their academic and professional goals.

Success Stories

"What inspired me to become a mentor is I come from an immigrant parent and I never got the help that I needed to go into higher education so I want to be that person to help guide others and not have them go through the hardships or mistakes that I went through to be or go to college."
