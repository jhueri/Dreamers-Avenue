Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in the subject line of your message.

Can I schedule a call or meeting?

Yes! Please send us an email or use the contact form to request a call or virtual meeting. We'll work with you to find a suitable time.

Do you offer in-person services?

We primarily operate virtually, but we do host in-person workshops and events in select locations throughout the year. Check our events calendar for upcoming opportunities.

How can I request a speaker for my event?

We're happy to consider speaking engagements. Please use the contact form and select "Partnerships" as the subject. Include details about your event, the audience, and the specific topics you'd like us to address.
