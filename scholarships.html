Scholarships for Undocumented Students

Explore a variety of scholarship opportunities designed to help you achieve your educational goals. This page features merit-based, need-based, and field-specific scholarships with key details on eligibility, application requirements, and award amounts. Start your journey toward a brighter future by applying to scholarships that match your qualifications!

The Dreamer Scholarship

By: The Dream Foundation

This scholarship supports undocumented students who have demonstrated academic achievement and community involvement.

Amount: $5,000

Eligibility: Open to all undocumented students pursuing a 2-year or 4-year degree.

The Unidos Scholarship

By: UnidosUS

UnidosUS provides financial support for undocumented students who are committed to advocacy and leadership in their communities.

Amount: $2,500

Eligibility: Must demonstrate financial need and be involved in social justice initiatives.
