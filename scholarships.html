Scholarships for Undocumented Students

Explore a variety of scholarship opportunities designed to help you achieve your educational goals. This page features merit-based, need-based, and field-specific scholarships with key details on eligibility, application requirements, and award amounts. Start your journey toward a brighter future by applying to scholarships that match your qualifications!

Cooke Undergraduate Transfer Scholarship

By: Jack Kent Cooke Foundation

Each award is intended to cover a significant share of the student’s educational expenses – including tuition, living expenses, books and required fees – for the final two to three years necessary to achieve a bachelor’s degree. Awards vary by individual, based on the cost of tuition as well as other grants or scholarships they may receive.

Amount: Up to $55,000

Deadline: 01/09/2025

Apply Now

The Unidos Scholarship

By: UnidosUS

UnidosUS provides financial support for undocumented students who are committed to advocacy and leadership in their communities.

Amount: $2,500

Deadline: Must demonstrate financial need and be involved in social justice initiatives.
