About Dreamers Avenue

Learn about our mission, story, and the team behind our organization

Our Mission

Dreamers Avenue is dedicated to empowering undocumented high school students to pursue higher education by providing mentorship, resources, and community support throughout the college application process.

We believe that every student, regardless of immigration status, deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to achieve their academic and professional goals.

Our Story

Dreamers Avenue began as an Instagram blog sharing tips, resources, and encouragement for undocumented high school students applying to college.

What started as a few posts aimed at making the process less confusing quickly grew into a trusted space for students across the country. As more students reached out, it became clear that the need was bigger and so was the opportunity to help.

Since then, Dreamers Avenue has evolved into a national platform offering mentorship, scholarship guides, student ambassador programs, and more. Every step of the way, it has remained grounded in the same mission: making higher education more accessible for undocumented students.

Rooted in community, driven by student voices, and powered by lived experience, Dreamers Avenue continues to grow as a space where students are seen, supported, and empowered.
