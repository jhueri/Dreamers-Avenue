About Dreamers Avenue

Learn about our mission, story, and the team behind our organization

Our Mission

Dreamers Avenue is dedicated to empowering undocumented high school students to pursue higher education by providing mentorship, resources, and community support throughout the college application process.

We believe that every student, regardless of immigration status, deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to achieve their academic and professional goals.

Our Story

Dreamers Avenue was founded in 2015 by a group of first-generation college graduates who experienced firsthand the challenges undocumented students face when applying to college.

What began as informal mentoring sessions at a local community center has grown into a comprehensive support program that has helped hundreds of undocumented students navigate the complex college application process and secure financial aid.

Our founders recognized that undocumented students face unique barriers to higher education, including limited access to federal financial aid, fear of disclosing immigration status, and lack of guidance from school counselors who may not be familiar with the specific challenges these students face.

Today, Dreamers Avenue continues to grow its impact by expanding mentorship programs, developing new resources, and advocating for policies that support undocumented students' access to higher education.
