Your Impact

Every donation, no matter the size, makes a meaningful difference in the lives of undocumented students. Your support helps us provide personalized mentorship, college application guidance, and essential resources that open doors to higher education.

Together, we're breaking down barriers and creating pathways to success for students who deserve every opportunity to pursue their dreams.

Stories of Impact

"Thanks to Dreamers Avenue's support, I was able to navigate the complex college application process and secure scholarships. The mentorship I received was invaluable in helping me achieve my dream of higher education."

- Former Mentee, Now College Student

"What inspired me to become a mentor is I come from an immigrant parent and I never got the help that I needed to go into higher education so I want to be that person to help guide others and not have them go through the hardships or mistakes that I went through to be or go to college."

- Johanna T., Volunteer Mentor
