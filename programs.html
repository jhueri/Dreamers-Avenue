Mentorship Program

At Dreamers Avenue, our mentorship program is designed to guide undocumented students through the complex college application process by pairing them with mentors who offer personalized support, advice, and encouragement. Whether you're looking for help with college applications, essays, scholarships, or emotional support, our mentors are here to assist you every step of the way.

How It Works

  • Personalized Mentor Matching: We carefully match each student (mentee) with a mentor based on their needs, goals, and intended college major or field of interest.
  • One-on-One Guidance: Mentors work directly with mentees to help them navigate college applications, find scholarship opportunities, and offer academic guidance and emotional support.
  • Flexible Communication: Mentors and mentees can communicate via email, video calls, or other online platforms to ensure regular and meaningful interactions.

Eligibility

Mentees

Must be an undocumented student in high school or an incoming college freshman seeking support with college applications, financial aid, and scholarships.

Mentors

Should have experience with the college application process and a strong understanding of the unique challenges that undocumented students encounter in accessing higher education.
